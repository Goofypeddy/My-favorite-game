My Favorite Video Game

Warframe is a pseudo MMORPG made by Digital Extremes(DE). The game first when live in March of 2013. Since it's release it has been developed into a game that is practically unrecognizable from what it was.
Official game site:Warframe.com.

The Marketplace

The Warframe Marketplace is a online, fan created, hub for ingame item and currency trading. To buy something in the marketplace the user simply needs to click on the search bar and type in the item name. once they have reach their desired item, they can check prices and quanitys of each seller to chose which seller to buy from. To get the item ingame they user needs to click on the buy botton and copy the users message link into the ingame chat. Then the player trades the item at a clan hub with the seller in exchange for platinum(the ingame currency bought for real money or more commonly earned through ingame trading).
